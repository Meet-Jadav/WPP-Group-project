Chandrayaan-1 Mission Announced
On August 15, 2003, India officially announced its plan to send a mission to the moon.

This historic step marked India’s entry into lunar exploration through ISRO.
Gujarat Earthquake Rehabilitation
Throughout 2003, large-scale reconstruction continued in Kutch after the 2001 quake.

The government rebuilt homes, roads, and infrastructure across affected districts.
India-Pakistan Cricket Ties Revived
In November 2003, India agreed to resume cricketing ties with Pakistan.

This move was seen as a diplomatic breakthrough and a people-to-people initiative.
Telecom Reforms Strengthened
In October 2003, the government approved policies for Unified Access Services.

This move opened the path for affordable mobile connectivity across India.
Delhi Metro Phase I Expands
In December 2003, major new stretches of the Delhi Metro were opened to the public.

The Metro improved public transport and reduced traffic congestion in Delhi.
Golden Quadrilateral Highway Progress
By mid-2003, over 40% of the Golden Quadrilateral highway network was completed.

This massive project aimed to connect India’s major cities via modern highways.
