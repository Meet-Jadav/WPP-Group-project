Rewind through 2000
Jharkhand Becomes a State
On November 15, 2000, Jharkhand was carved out of Bihar as India’s 28th state.

This marked the culmination of decades of demand for a separate tribal state.
India Releases Draft Nuclear Doctrine
On August 17, 2000, India unveiled a formal nuclear policy draft.

The doctrine included a 'no first use' policy and a credible minimum deterrent.
Preparations Begin for Census 2001
In late 2000, India began pre-enumeration work for the decadal Census.

This included mapping households and training enumerators across the country.
First Cyber Crime Police Station Opened
In 2000, Bengaluru opened India’s first police station dedicated to cyber crimes.

It marked a step forward in tackling online fraud and digital threats.
BSE Launches Online Trading
In February 2000, the Bombay Stock Exchange fully adopted online trading systems.

It significantly improved transparency and investor access to stock markets.
Priyanka Chopra Wins Miss World
On November 30, 2000, Priyanka Chopra won the Miss World crown in London.

She became the fifth Indian to win the title and later a global icon.
