Chandrayaan-3 Moon Landing
On August 23, 2023, India became the first country to land on the Moon's south pole.

ISRO’s Chandrayaan-3 mission was a historic success, making India the 4th nation to achieve a soft landing on the Moon.
India Hosts G20 Summit
India hosted the G20 Summit in New Delhi on September 9–10, 2023.

Leaders from around the world gathered as India emphasized themes like climate action, digital economy, and global south cooperation.
Women’s Reservation Bill Passed
On September 21, 2023, Parliament passed the Women’s Reservation Bill.

The bill reserves 33% of seats for women in the Lok Sabha and state assemblies — a historic move for political representation.
INDIA Alliance Formed
In July 2023, opposition parties formed the INDIA alliance to challenge the ruling party in the 2024 elections.

The acronym stands for Indian National Developmental Inclusive Alliance and includes over 20 political parties.
India’s Historic Medal Tally at Asian Games
In October 2023, India achieved its best-ever performance at the Asian Games in Hangzhou, China.

India crossed the 100-medal mark for the first time, finishing with 107 medals: 28 gold, 38 silver, and 41 bronze.
Cheetah Reintroduction Project Faces Challenges
In 2023, several African cheetahs relocated to India’s Kuno National Park died.

While the ambitious conservation project continued, concerns were raised over adaptation and management issues.
