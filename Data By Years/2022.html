Rewind through 2022
75th Independence Anniversary
On August 15, 2022, India celebrated 75 years of independence with 'Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav'.

Massive celebrations took place across the country with the “Har Ghar Tiranga” campaign and cultural events.
Droupadi Murmu Becomes President
On July 25, 2022, Droupadi Murmu took oath as the 15th President of India.

She became the **first tribal woman** and the **youngest-ever President** of India.
India Overtakes UK as 5th Largest Economy
In September 2022, India surpassed the UK to become the world’s 5th largest economy.

According to IMF data, India moved ahead in terms of nominal GDP despite global economic slowdowns.
India Wins Test Series in England
In July 2022, India beat England at Lord’s in the rescheduled 5th Test.

India won the series 2-1, showcasing dominance in overseas Test cricket.
Delhi Air Pollution Crisis
In November 2022, Delhi faced hazardous air quality levels, touching AQI values above 450.

Schools were shut, and construction activities were halted under GRAP emergency measures.
India at Commonwealth Games
From July 28 to August 8, 2022, India bagged **61 medals** in Birmingham.

The haul included 22 gold, with standout performances in wrestling, weightlifting, and athletics.
