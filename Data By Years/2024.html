Ram Mandir Inauguration
On January 22, 2024, the Ram Mandir in Ayodhya was inaugurated, marking a significant cultural and religious milestone in India.

The grand ceremony was attended by thousands, including Prime Minister Narendra Modi, and symbolized the culmination of a centuries-old movement.
2024 Indian General Election
Held from April 19 to June 1, 2024, the general elections saw a record voter turnout of over 642 million people.

The BJP-led NDA secured 293 seats, and Narendra Modi was sworn in for a third term as Prime Minister on June 9, 2024.
India Wins 2024 T20 World Cup
On June 29, 2024, India clinched the ICC Men's T20 World Cup by defeating South Africa in the final held in Barbados.

This victory marked India's second T20 World Cup title and was the final T20 match for veterans Virat Kohli, Rohit Sharma, and Ravindra Jadeja.
Vibrant Gujarat Global Summit 2024
From January 10–12, 2024, the 10th Vibrant Gujarat Summit was held in Gandhinagar, attracting global leaders and investors.

The summit focused on sectors like semiconductors, green hydrogen, and renewable energy, resulting in MoUs worth ₹26.33 lakh crore.
India-Canada Diplomatic Row
In October 2024, Canada expelled six Indian diplomats over allegations linked to the murder of Sikh separatist Hardeep Singh Nijjar.

India denied the allegations and responded by expelling Canadian diplomats, leading to a significant strain in bilateral relations.
India-China Border Agreement
In July 2024, India and China reached an agreement to disengage troops along the Line of Actual Control (LAC) after prolonged tensions.

The pact aimed to reduce military presence and prevent further clashes in the disputed Himalayan border regions.
