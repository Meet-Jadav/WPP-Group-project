Article 370 Abrogated
On August 5, 2019, the Indian government revoked Article 370, removing the special status of Jammu and Kashmir.

Jammu and Kashmir was reorganized into two Union Territories: Jammu & Kashmir and Ladakh.
Balakot Airstrike
On February 26, 2019, India conducted airstrikes on a terrorist training camp in Balakot, Pakistan.

The strike was in response to the Pulwama terror attack that killed 40 CRPF personnel.
Chandrayaan-2 Mission
On July 22, 2019, ISRO launched Chandrayaan-2, India’s second lunar exploration mission.

Though the Vikram lander lost contact during descent, the orbiter continues to function successfully.
COVID-19 Outbreak Begins
In December 2019, the first cases of a novel coronavirus (COVID-19) were reported in Wuhan, China.

The outbreak would later be declared a global pandemic by the WHO in March 2020.
Amazon Rainforest Fires
During mid-2019, massive wildfires in the Amazon rainforest drew global concern.

These fires raised awareness about deforestation and environmental policies in Brazil.
TikTok Boom
By 2019, TikTok had become one of the most downloaded apps globally, especially among Gen Z users.

The app reshaped entertainment and became a major cultural phenomenon worldwide.
