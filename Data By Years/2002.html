Gujarat Riots
Riots erupted on February 27, 2002, after the Godhra train burning incident.

The violence led to over 1,000 deaths and drew national and international attention.
Narmada Bachao Andolan Intensifies
In March 2002, protests grew against raising the height of the Sardar Sarovar Dam.

Activists demanded proper rehabilitation for displaced villagers.
Delhi Metro Trial Run Begins
On December 24, 2002, the Delhi Metro began its first public trial run.

It marked a new era in urban transportation in India.
A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Becomes President
On July 25, 2002, Dr. Kalam was sworn in as the 11th President of India.

He became known as the "People's President" and inspired millions.
Parliament Attack Trial Verdict
On December 18, 2002, the special court delivered verdicts in the 2001 attack case.

Four people were convicted, including Afzal Guru who was sentenced to death.
Launch of National Health Mission Planning
In late 2002, groundwork began for what would become the NRHM in 2005.

The aim was to improve rural healthcare infrastructure and access.

Year 2002: A year of tragedy, leadership, and infrastructural milestones in India
