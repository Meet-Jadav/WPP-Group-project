India Hosts Commonwealth Games
On October 3, 2010, Delhi hosted the 19th Commonwealth Games, marking a major international sporting event in India.

Despite early controversies and delays, the games were successfully held with India winning 101 medals and finishing second overall. The event showcased India’s sporting talent and infrastructure development.
Ayodhya Verdict Delivered
On September 30, 2010, the Allahabad High Court delivered its much-anticipated verdict on the Ayodhya land dispute.

The court divided the disputed land into three parts among the Sunni Waqf Board, Nirmohi Akhara, and Ram Lalla. The verdict was seen as an attempt to balance religious claims and maintain peace.
Apple Launches First iPad
On April 3, 2010, Apple launched the iPad, revolutionizing personal computing and media consumption.

The sleek touchscreen tablet blended portability and productivity, creating a new product category that inspired competitors and transformed tech habits worldwide.
Facebook Reaches 500 Million Users
On July 21, 2010, Facebook crossed the milestone of 500 million active users globally.

This marked a key point in the social media revolution, reflecting how digital communication was reshaping daily life and global connections. India was among the fastest-growing user bases.
3 Idiots Becomes a Global Hit
In January 2010, *3 Idiots* became the highest-grossing Bollywood film ever, both in India and internationally.

The film sparked conversations about India’s education system and became a cultural phenomenon, even gaining popularity in countries like China and South Korea.
Burj Khalifa Opens to the Public
On January 4, 2010, the Burj Khalifa was officially opened in Dubai, becoming the tallest building in the world.

Standing at 828 meters, it symbolized architectural ambition and luxury in the Middle East. It also became a global tourist attraction and cultural icon.
India Wins Asia Cup
On June 24, 2010, India defeated Sri Lanka in the final to win the Asia Cup cricket tournament.

The win was India’s first Asia Cup title in 15 years and a big morale booster ahead of the 2011 World Cup. Gautam Gambhir and MS Dhoni were key performers.

Year 2010: A year of powerful events in politics, entertainment, environment, science, and global change.
