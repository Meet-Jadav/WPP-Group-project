BJP Wins Delhi Assembly Elections
On February 5, 2025, BJP returned to power in Delhi after 27 years, winning 48 out of 70 seats.

Rekha Gupta was sworn in as Chief Minister, marking a significant political shift in the capital.
Maha Kumbh Mela 2025
Held in Prayagraj from January 13 to February 26, the Maha Kumbh drew over 660 million devotees.

It became one of the largest religious gatherings in the world, centered around ritual bathing at the Triveni Sangam.
New Delhi Railway Station Tragedy
On February 15, 2025, a crowd crush at the railway station caused 18 deaths and 15 injuries.

The incident was linked to overcrowding due to pilgrims traveling to the Maha Kumbh Mela.
India Wins Kho Kho World Cup
India hosted and won the inaugural Kho Kho World Cup in January 2025 in New Delhi.

Both the men’s and women’s teams defeated Nepal in the finals, showcasing India’s excellence in the indigenous sport.
India Wins ICC Champions Trophy
India won the ICC Champions Trophy 2025, defeating Australia in a thrilling final.

The final was held in London, and India secured victory with a stunning performance from both bat and ball, earning global cricket glory once again.
US Vice President Visits India
In April 2025, VP JD Vance visited India to discuss trade, defense, and Indo-Pacific partnerships.

The visit aimed to strengthen economic ties and propose a bilateral trade target of $500 billion by 2030.
India Considers Nuclear Law Reform
India announced plans to amend its Civil Nuclear Liability Act to attract foreign nuclear investors.

The move aims to expand India’s nuclear energy capacity to 100 GW by 2047 by easing liability clauses.
