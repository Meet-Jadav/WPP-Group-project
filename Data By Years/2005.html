Irfan Pathan’s Test Hat-trick
On January 29, 2005 Irfan Pathan took a sensational hat-trick in the first over of a Test match against Pakistan in Karachi.

He became the first bowler to achieve this feat in the opening over of a Test match.
Manmohan Singh’s Leadership
Prime Minister Manmohan Singh continued his second year in office, focusing on economic growth.

His government prioritized rural development, education, and social welfare schemes like the NREGA (passed in 2005).
Introduction of VAT
On April 1, 2005 India rolled out Value Added Tax (VAT) in 21 states, replacing the complex sales tax system.

It aimed to streamline indirect taxation and improve compliance.
RTI Act Enacted
On June 15, 2005 The RTI Act was passed by the Indian Parliament and came into effect in October.

It empowered citizens to seek information from public authorities, enhancing transparency.
London Bombings
On July 7, 2005 Four coordinated suicide bomb attacks struck London’s public transport during rush hour.

The bombings resulted in 52 civilian deaths and over 700 injuries.
YouTube Launched
YouTube was founded as a platform for sharing user-generated videos online.

It would go on to redefine internet culture and digital content forever.
