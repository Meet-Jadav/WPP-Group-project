Rewind through 2013
Telangana Statehood Approved
On July 30, 2013, the Congress Working Committee approved the formation of Telangana as India’s 29th state.

The decision came after years of protests, hunger strikes, and political debates. The bifurcation was officially finalized in 2014, but the 2013 approval was a landmark moment in Indian political history.
ISRO Launches IRNSS-1A
On July 1, 2013, ISRO launched IRNSS-1A — India’s first regional navigation satellite.

It marked India’s step toward building its own GPS-like navigation system, boosting national autonomy in strategic sectors and establishing a foundation for future regional navigation services.
Launch of PlayStation 4 and Xbox One
In November 2013, Sony and Microsoft released their next-gen consoles just a week apart.

This marked the beginning of a new gaming era with advanced graphics, cloud gaming integration, and social connectivity. These systems laid the foundation for modern console gaming as we know it today.
Narendra Modi Named BJP’s Prime Ministerial Candidate
BJP officially declared Modi as its PM candidate for the 2014 elections in September 2013.

This announcement was a defining moment in Indian politics. Modi's development-focused campaign and governance record as Gujarat’s Chief Minister set the tone for a nationwide movement that would lead to a historic win the following year.
India Wins ICC Champions Trophy
India beat England in a rain-shortened final to win the ICC Champions Trophy.

The win came under the leadership of M.S. Dhoni, making India the first team to win all three major ICC trophies. The victory added to India's dominance in global cricket and energized fans worldwide.
Sachin Tendulkar Retires from International Cricket
On November 16, 2013, Tendulkar played his 200th and final Test match.

The farewell match at Wankhede Stadium was an emotional tribute to a career spanning 24 years. Widely regarded as one of the greatest cricketers in history, Tendulkar's retirement marked the end of an iconic era in Indian sports.
U.S. Government Shutdown
A budget deadlock in Congress led to a partial government shutdown.

From October 1 to 17, 2013, over 800,000 federal employees were furloughed, and many services were suspended. The shutdown sparked political turmoil and renewed debates over healthcare reform and fiscal responsibility.
India Launches Mars Orbiter Mission (Mangalyaan)
On November 5, ISRO launched its first interplanetary mission to Mars.

The Mars Orbiter Mission (MOM) made India the first Asian country — and the first in the world — to reach Mars on its maiden attempt. The low-cost mission was praised worldwide for its engineering excellence and ambition.
